## üéØ Targeting Strategy

### Facebook Targeting

**Demographics:**
- Age: 30-60
- Household income: Top 25%
- Life events: Recently engaged, newlywed, anniversary
- Job titles: Manager, Director, VP, Owner

**Interests:**
- Travel: Expedia, TripAdvisor, Travel + Leisure
- Destinations: Caribbean, Europe, Hawaii, Mexico
- Travel type: Luxury travel, cruise vacations, resort stays
- Lifestyle: Fine dining, wine, golf, spa

**Behaviors:**
- Frequent travelers
- Online purchasers
- Luxury goods buyers
- Mobile users

**Custom Audiences:**
- Website visitors (last 90 days)
- Email list (existing customers)
- Engaged with Facebook/Instagram posts

**Lookalike Audiences:**
- 1% lookalike of payment completers (highest value)
- 2% lookalike of planning fee acceptors
- 5% lookalike of qualified leads

### Google Ads Targeting

**Search Keywords:**

**High Intent (High Bids):**
- [travel planning service] - Exact match
- [custom trip planner] - Exact match
- [vacation planning help] - Phrase match
- [honeymoon planner] - Exact match

**Medium Intent (Medium Bids):**
- "travel concierge service" - Phrase match
- "help planning vacation" - Phrase match
- "custom travel itinerary" - Phrase match

**Low Intent (Low Bids):**
- +travel +planning +service - Modified broad
- Caribbean vacation planner - Broad match

**Audiences (Layering):**
- In-market: Travel planning
- In-market: Luxury travel
- Custom intent: Travel search history
- Affinity: Travel buffs, frequent travelers
- Remarketing: Website visitors

**Geographic Targeting:**
- Start: Nationwide (US only)
- Optimize: Focus on top-performing states
- Typical winners: CA, TX, FL, NY, IL

## üìù Creative Strategy

### Facebook Creative Approach

**Ad Format Priority:**
1. **Carousel Ads** (best performing)
   - 3-5 destination images
   - Each card = different benefit
   - Final card = CTA

2. **Video Ads** (highest engagement)
   - 15-30 second clips
   - Beautiful destination B-roll
   - Text overlay with benefits

3. **Single Image** (easiest to test)
   - Hero destination photo
   - Clear value proposition
   - Strong CTA button

**Creative Testing Matrix:**
- 3 different images
- 3 different headlines
- 2 different descriptions
- = 18 variations to test

**Refresh Schedule:**
- New creative every 14 days
- Retire ads with >$15 CPL
- Scale winners with increased budget

### Google Ads Creative Approach

**Responsive Search Ads:**
- 15 unique headlines (mix benefits + features)
- 4 unique descriptions (mix urgency + value)
- Pin headline 1 for brand consistency
- Let Google optimize the rest

**Ad Copy Themes:**
1. **Urgency**: "Get Called in 60 Seconds"
2. **Value**: "Save 40-70% on Travel"
3. **Service**: "Expert Travel Planning"
4. **Benefit**: "Custom Itineraries"
5. **Social Proof**: "Rated 4.9 Stars"

**Display/YouTube:**
- Responsive display ads (auto-optimize)
- Bumper ads (6 seconds) for remarketing
- TrueView for reach (awareness)

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Facebook Mistakes

‚ùå **Targeting too broad**
- Solution: Start with detailed targeting, expand later

‚ùå **Not refreshing creative**
- Solution: New images every 2 weeks

‚ùå **Ignoring ad frequency**
- Solution: Pause ads with >3.0 frequency

‚ùå **Optimizing too early**
- Solution: Wait for 50+ conversions before major changes

### Google Ads Mistakes

‚ùå **Too many keywords at launch**
- Solution: Start with 20-30 high-intent keywords

‚ùå **Broad match keywords**
- Solution: Use exact and phrase match until you have data

‚ùå **Not using negative keywords**
- Solution: Add 10-20 negatives in first week

‚ùå **Competing with yourself**
- Solution: Avoid keyword overlap between campaigns

## üéØ Quick Start Guide

### If You Can Only Choose One:

**Choose Facebook If:**
- Budget under $1,000/month
- Need results this week
- Have great visual assets
- First time running ads

**Choose Google If:**
- Budget over $1,500/month
- Can wait 2-3 weeks for data
- Want highest quality leads
- Experienced with PPC

### Recommended Launch Sequence:

**Week 1:** Launch Facebook ($300)
- Get first leads flowing
- Test consent messaging
- Validate AI callback system

**Week 2:** Optimize Facebook, Launch Google ($600 total)
- Scale Facebook winners
- Start Google learning phase
- Compare lead quality

**Week 3-4:** Split Testing ($900-1,200 total)
- Find optimal budget split
- Test cross-platform messaging
- Identify best lead sources

**Month 2+:** Full Deployment ($1,500-3,000+)
- Run both platforms at scale
- Continuous optimization
- Expand to new audiences/keywords
